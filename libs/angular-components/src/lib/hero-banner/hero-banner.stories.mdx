## Overview

A large image area used to give visual reinforcement of a particular section of content.

## Examples

```html
<goa-hero-banner
    backgroundUrl="backgroundUrl"
    title="Upgrading our bitumen">
  <goa-hero-banner-content>
    As part of our Made-in-Alberta energy strategy, a partial upgrader will be built in the Industrial Heartland â€“
    creating more jobs and adding more value to our resources.
  </goa-hero-banner-content>
  <goa-hero-banner-link url="/partial-upgrading-program.aspx">
</goa-hero-banner>
```
