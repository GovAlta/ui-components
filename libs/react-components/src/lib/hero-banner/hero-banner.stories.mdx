## Overview

A large image area used to give visual reinforcement of a particular section of content.

## Examples

### Passing properties

```html
<GoAHeroBanner
  title="Upgrading our bitumen"
  backgroundUrl="..."
  content="Resources are available to help ..."
>
</GoAHeroBanner>
```

### Passing child elements

```html
<GoAHeroBanner title="Upgrading our bitumen" backgroundUrl="..." }>
</GoAHeroBanner>
```
