# Footer

The footer resides at the bottom of every page of your service, providing copyright information and other information related to your service.

[Figma design component](https://www.figma.com/file/3pb2IK8s2QUqWieH79KdN7/%E2%9D%96-Components-%7C-DDI?node-id=5652%3A228700)

---

#### Meta properties

<Props>
  <Prop
    name="slot"
    type="meta"
    description="Anchor elements to display in the bottom meta section"
  />
</Props>

#### Secondary navigation properties

<Props>
  <Prop
    name="maxcolumncount"
    type="number"
    defaultValue="1"
    description="Allow links to be distributed over the set value of columns on higher resolutions"
  />
  <Prop
    name="slot"
    type="nav"
    description="Anchor elements to display in the top navigation section"
  />
</Props>
