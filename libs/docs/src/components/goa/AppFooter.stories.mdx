# Footer

The footer resides at the bottom of every page of your service, providing copyright information and other information related to your service.
The footer will not position itself on the bottom of the page, but must instead be positioned by either a GoA or custom layout component.

[Figma design component](https://www.figma.com/file/3pb2IK8s2QUqWieH79KdN7/%E2%9D%96-Components-%7C-DDI?node-id=5652%3A228700)

---

    name="slot"
    lang="angular"

</Props>

#### Secondary navigation properties

<Props showTabs={true}>
  <Prop
    name="maxColumnCount"
    type="number"
    defaultValue="1"
    description="Set the number of columns for navigation links on larger viewports"
  />
  <Prop
    name="heading"
    type="string"
    description="Sub heading of the navigation links"
  />
  <Prop
    name="slot"
    lang="angular"
    type="nav"
    description="Anchor elements to display in the top navigation section"
  />
</Props>
