<goa-text as="h2" id="why-start-with-the-design-system" size="heading-m">Why start with the design system?</goa-text>

<goa-text size="heading-xs">Streamline collaboration</goa-text>
<p>
  Designers and developers work from shared components and guidance. This reduces rework and misalignment.
</p>

<goa-text size="heading-xs">Improve accessibility</goa-text>
<p>Accessibility is built into both the design and coded components.</p>

<goa-text size="heading-xs">Reduce testing effort</goa-text>
<p>Components are tested across devices, browsers, and service contexts.</p>

<goa-text size="heading-xs">Maintain consistency</goa-text>
<p>
  Components align with the broader system to create a cohesive user experience across government services.
</p>

<goa-text size="heading-xs">Spend more time on high-value work</goa-text>
<p>Using existing components saves time. You can focus on work that improves service quality, including:</p>
<ul>
  <li>Usability testing</li>
  <li>User research</li>
  <li>Content design</li>
  <li>Accessibility reviews</li>
  <li>Design quality checks</li>
  <li>Low-fidelity prototyping and testing</li>
</ul>
<p>This leads to better outcomes for users.</p>
<goa-text as="h2" id="how-to-use-the-design-system" size="heading-m">How to use the design system in your service</goa-text>
<p>Follow these steps when designing and building your service.</p>

<goa-text size="heading-xs">Use system components first</goa-text>
<p>Start with existing components and patterns. These meet most user needs.</p>

<goa-text size="heading-xs">Identify gaps through user testing</goa-text>
<p>Test your service with users. Identify needs that are not covered by current components.</p>

<goa-text size="heading-xs">Before you design something new</goa-text>
<p>
  Check what's already available and what's in progress. The team can point you to existing components,
  solutions other teams have built, and planned work that may meet your need.
</p>

<goa-text size="heading-xs">Design and test new solutions when you need them</goa-text>
<p>
  If you still have a gap, design a new solution and test it with users. Move forward only when
  testing shows clear user value.
</p>

<goa-text size="heading-xs">Share what you learn</goa-text>
<p>
  Share your findings with the design system team. Your solution may become part of the design system.
</p>

<goa-callout version="2" type="important" emphasis="low" heading="Avoid unnecessary custom solutions" mt="xl" mb="m">
  <goa-text size="body-m" mt="2xs" mb="s">Do not create custom components without a clear user need.</goa-text>
  <goa-text size="body-m" mt="none" mb="s">Unnecessary customization:</goa-text>
  <ul style={{ marginBottom: "var(--goa-space-s)" }}>
    <li>Increases maintenance effort</li>
    <li>Slows delivery</li>
    <li>Reduces consistency</li>
  </ul>
  <goa-text size="body-m" mt="none" mb="none">Always confirm the need through research and testing.</goa-text>
</goa-callout>
